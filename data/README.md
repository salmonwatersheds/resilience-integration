# `data/`

* The most raw form of the data goes in here
* Treat as read-only
* Processed data go in `outputs/`

## Metadata

* `dataset556_cu_habitat_threats.csv` - Habitat Assessment Threat Summaries for Salmon and Steelhead Conservation Units downloaded from the Data Library: https://data.salmonwatersheds.ca/result?datasetid=556

* `exposure_overall.csv` from [Online Supplement to Peacock et al. (in review)](https://salmonwatersheds.github.io/ccva/), giving the overall exposure to climate changes across the life cycle under 6 different Global Climate Models (we use the median exposure among GCMs in subdsequent analyses).
